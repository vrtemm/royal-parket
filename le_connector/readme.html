Following is a step-by-step guide for those who are not a big fan of watching videos.

The procedure is the same for macOS, Windows and Linux users. You can use FileZilla or any other FTP applications (WinSCP, FileFTP, CuteFTP).

  • If you haven’t had an FTP client yet, you can have it set up with a few simple steps. Please refer to this video for a complete guide to FTP installation.

  • If you don’t have FTP access details, contact your hosting provider or developer to get them. In case they are unable to help, feel free to contact our 24/7 support team.

Step 1: At your Migration Setup page, after providing your Source and Target Cart URLs, you can easily download the connector by clicking "Download LitExtension Connector". You will receive a zip file named "le_connector".

Step 2: Unzip the "le_connector" file and upload it to your Source and Target stores’ root folders via FTP access. Whichever FTP client you are using (FileZilla, WinSCP, FileFTP, CuteFTP), enter the root folder of your store and upload the "le_connector" folder to it.

You can follow the guideline about root folder and how to find your store’s root folder here.

Note: For Wordpress (WooCommerce, Shopp, WP e-Commerce) or Joomla (AceShop, JooCart, VirtueMart, MijoShop) e-commerce plugins the "le_connector" folder should be uploaded to the root directory where your CMS (Wordpress/Joomla) is installed, not your e-commerce plugin directory.

Step 3: After uploading the connector, here’s how to check whether it has been installed correctly:

  • Click on the instructed link.

  • Type into your browser: http://YourStoreUrl/le_connector/connector.php.

If you see the message "Connector Installed" show up, the connector has been installed properly and you can proceed to the next step of your migration. Otherwise, the connector installation has failed due to permission-related errors. To fix this, please set the permission for your store’s root folder as follows:

  • "connector.php" - 644 or 666, depending on your server configuration;

  • "/le_connector" folder - 755 or 777, depending on your server configuration.

Or else, don’t hesitate to contact our support team for further assistance.

Step 4: In order to copy images during your migration, make sure to set the "write" permission for the image directory and ALL the subfolders/files it contains on your Target Store (chmod 777).
